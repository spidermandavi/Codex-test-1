Compatibility format

  • schemaVersion lets future importers recognize this file shape.
  • chapters[].moveTree stores mainline moves as children and variations as sibling branches.
  • path is a stable SAN sequence, so future files can merge identical lines by path.
  • comments and nags stay attached to the exact move node they came from.
